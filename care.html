About Us

Caring Hearts is a non-profit organization under the pax romana chaplaincy that aims to help the less fortunate within and outside our chaplaincy. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to live a better life. We provide food, shelter, and other necessities to those in need.Also we provide support to our chaplaincy when the need arises. Our vision is to make the world a better place for us to live together beautifully. We have a mission with a vision. We appeal to you to support us in anyway you can.
